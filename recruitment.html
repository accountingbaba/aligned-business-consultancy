The Difference

Most agencies match keywords.
We assess competence.

Most agencies treat hiring as a matching exercise: they read a CV, match keywords to a job description, and send you a pile of profiles. What they can't do is tell you whether the candidate actually knows their job.

We can. Our team understands the roles we recruit for, not as outsiders reading a brief, but as practitioners who know what competence looks like at each level. That's the difference in the quality of our shortlist.

How We Work

Five steps from brief to
successful placement.

01
A conversation, not a job description

Tell us what success looks like. Tell us what's gone wrong with previous hires. That context shapes everything we do next.

02
Source and screen with depth

Active candidate network plus targeted outreach. Every candidate goes through structured competency assessment before you see them.

03
A shortlist, not a pile of CVs

Three to five candidates, with our written assessment on each: what we found, what we recommend, what to watch.

04
Interview process support

Scheduling, role-specific interview questions, and an evaluation framework so every candidate is assessed consistently.

05
Stay through the finish line

Offer management, negotiation support, and a 30-day check-in after joining to make sure the placement lands well.
